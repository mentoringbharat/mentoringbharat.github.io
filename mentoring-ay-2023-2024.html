Our Mission

Purpose of Mentoring

Our goal is to support graduating IIT students by senior Alumni, providing a comprehensive safety net that bridges the gap between academic capability and industry employability.

CV Preparation & Review
Mock Interviews by Experts
Communication Skills
Job Search & Negotiation
Personal & Confidence Building
Access to Alumni Network
