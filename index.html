About Us

A professional networking platform for IIT-IIM-NIT alumni belonging to the Constitutionally marginalized Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes. The Group has over 1,000 alumni across the world and is fast growing.

Inspired by the social justice philosophy, the aim is to support fellow alumni's academic, professional and social progress as a means to pay back and uplift the community as a whole. The Group does advocacy work to improve diversity in higher education. It is non-political and works on trust, respect, integrity and community service spirit.

Mentoring Bharat Initiative

Started in 2023, following the unfortunate death of IIT Bombay's Darshan Solanki, this initiative extends mentoring support to IIT students coming from marginalized backgrounds. Contextual mentoring is provided by alumni who faced similar issues and are now thriving in their careers.

Impact: Over 1,000 IIT and NIT students have availed mentoring support through our network.
