Mission & Values

1,000+ Members

The Group is deeply inspired by the social justice philosophy. It is a space for alumni belonging to the Constitutionally marginalised Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.

"The playing field is not level for all. The mission is to support fellow alumni academic, professional and social progress as a means to pay back and uplift the community as a whole."

  • 1

    Advocacy & Representation

    The Group also does advocacy work to improve representation in various areas.

  • 2

    Core Values

    We are non-political and work on trust, respect, integrity, and community service spirit. The Group respects everyone in the world.
